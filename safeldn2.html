Welcome to Safer London

Safer London is a live map designed for Londoners to stay informed and updated about crimes happening in the local areas where they live and go.

This project uses the popular mapping tool MapBox to visualise detailed crime patterns and incidents across the city. This interactive map features several additional layers, including London borough boundaries, marked crime points, and attached popups for a more informative visualisation.

The data used in the map are collected from the UK's Police API, which provides the exact location, category, and outcome of each crime. Users are empowered to zoom in the London map and click on the marked points to know further details.

Journalists can leverage this map to pinpoint high-crime spots and areas in London and uncover valuable insights into crime trends when reporting related topics.
